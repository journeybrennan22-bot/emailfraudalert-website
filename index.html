Reply-To Hijacking

The "From" line looks right, but your reply goes to a criminal. We alert when your reply would go to a different address than the sender.

Invisible Characters

Cyrillic letters that look identical to English — impossible to spot with your eyes. Only software catches this.

Recently Created Domains

Scammers register domains hours before attacking. We check every sender's domain age — if it was created 3 days ago, that's not your bank.

Impersonation

Authentication Failures

Every legitimate sender passes SPF and DKIM checks. When an email claims to be from your bank but fails verification, we flag it.
