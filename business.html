Once the Wire Clears, the Bank Owes You Nothing.

Here's what most business owners don't know:

Under UCC 4A-207, banks are not legally required to verify that an account name matches an account number.

If your accounts payable team wires $150,000 to "First American Title" but the account number belongs to a criminal, the bank has fulfilled its legal duty.

The loss is yours. Not the bank's.

Recovery rate for international wire fraud: less than 1%.
